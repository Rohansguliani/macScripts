1. BETTERTOUCHTOOL - THREE FINGER DRAG FIX
============================================

Problem: Three-finger drag had a release delay when letting go.

Solution: BetterTouchTool automatically clicks after releasing three-finger drag.

Setup Steps:
1. Install BetterTouchTool from https://folivora.ai/
2. Go to Trackpad section in BTT
3. Add new trigger: "Release Last Finger After Previous Touch With 3 Fingers"
4. Assign action: "Left Click (At Current Mouse Position)"
5. Enable the trigger
6. (Optional) Disable macOS built-in three-finger drag in System Settings

This eliminates the delay by auto-clicking immediately after release.
